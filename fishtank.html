Fish Tank Recommendations

Starting with a second-hand 20-gallon tank is a great option for beginners!

5-Gallon Fish Tank

Learn More

Size: 16" x 8" x 10"

Suitable Fish: 1 Betta Fish

Tips: 25% water change every two to four weeks.

20-Gallon Fish Tank

Learn More

Size (High): 24" x 12" x 16"

Size (Long): 30" x 12" x 12"

Suitable Fish: Neon Tetras, Zebrafish, Guppy, Cherry Barb

Tips: 20% water change weekly.

50-Gallon Fish Tank

Learn More

Size: 36" x 18" x 19"

Suitable Fish: Comet Goldfish, Zebrafish, Neon Tetras, Cherry Barb

Tips: 10% to 20% water change every 1 to 2 weeks.

100-Gallon Fish Tank

Learn More

Size: 24" x 18" x 18"

Suitable Fish: Neon Tetras, Zebrafish, White Cloud Mountain Minnow, Guppy, Cherry Barb

Tips: 10% to 25% water change every 1 to 2 weeks.
