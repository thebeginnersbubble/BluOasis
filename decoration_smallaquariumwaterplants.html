Small Aquarium Water Plants

Marimo Moss Ball
Attachment Place directly on substrate (Do not bury)
Placement Foreground to midground
Substrate Type Fine Gravel or Coarse Sand
Aqueon Light Clip-on LED Light or OptiBright LED Light
Fertilizer Aqueon Plant Food
Java Moss
Attachment Secure to driftwood or rock
Placement Anywhere in tank, which is attached to hardscape
Substrate Type Driftwood or Porous Rock
Aqueon Light Clip-on LED Light or OptiBright LED Light
Fertilizer Aqueon Plant Food

Anubias Petite
Attachment Plant in substrate or secure to driftwood or rock
Placement Foreground or attached to hardscape
Substrate Type Sand or fine gravel, driftwood or Porous Rock
Aqueon Light Clip-on LED Light or OptiBright LED Light
Fertilizer Aqueon Plant Food or Root Tablets
