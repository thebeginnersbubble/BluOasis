Betta Splendens

Basic Profile
Family Belontiidae
Origin Southeast Asia: Cambodia, Thailand, Laos, Vietnam
Physical Characteristics Betta splendens have a torpedo-shaped body and an upturned mouth, perfect for surface feeding. Male Betta splendens are known for their brilliant coloration and long, flowing fins, while females have shorter fins by comparison.
Full-grown Size 3 inches
Life Span 3 to 5 years
Diet Carnivores
Tank Level All levels
Compatibility
Aggressive level Tank Mates
Unfortunately, because of the Betta Splendens' naturally aggressive personality, they aren't recommended to have tank mates.
Highly Aggressive
Fish Demands
Water Temperature 75 F to 86 F (24 C to 30 C)
Water Hardness Up to 20 dGH
pH Preferences pH 6.8 to pH 7.4
Minimum Tank Size 2 gallons
Feeding Betta splendens feed on insect larvae, as well as protein-based frozen, pellet, and flake foods.

Estimated Price (RM): 5-10 per Betta Splendens

Tip:

For a betta tank, it’s best to keep only 1 male, but you can safely house 2 female bettas together.

Fun Fact:

Bettas are known to recognize their human companions, will follow your finger around the aquarium, and can even be trained to perform tricks!
