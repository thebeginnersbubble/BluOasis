Zebrafish

Basic Profile
Family Cyprinidae
Origin Eastern India
Physical Characteristics Zebrafish feature blue-purple horizontal stripes that run from their gills to their tails, along with two pairs of barbels. Males are generally smaller, slender, and torpedo-shaped, while females are larger and have a fuller belly.
Full-grown Size 2 inches
Life Span 5 years
Diet Omnivore
Tank Level All levels
Compatibility
Aggressive level Tank Mates
Peaceful Comet Goldfish Neon Tetra Cherry Barb
Fish Demands
Water Temperature 64 F to 74 F (18 C to 24 C)
Water Hardness 5 dGH to 12 dGH
pH Preferences pH 6.5 to pH 7.0
Minimum Tank Size 10 gallons
Feeding Zebrafish enjoy small pelleted or flake fish food, along with freeze-dried brine shrimp, tubifex worms, and various frozen foods.

Estimated Price (RM): 2-5 per Zebrafish

Note:

Zebrafish may nip the fins of species with long, flowing fins, such as Angelfish, Bettas, and Guppies.
