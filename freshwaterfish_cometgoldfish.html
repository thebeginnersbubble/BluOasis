Comet Goldfish

Basic Profile
Family Cyprinidae
Origin Asia
Physical Characteristics Comet goldfish are long-bodied goldfish, typically displaying red, white, gold, or brown coloration.
Full-grown Size 12 to 14 inches
Life Span 10 to 20 years
Diet Omnivore
Tank Level Top and middle dwellers
Compatibility
Aggressive level Tank Mates
Peaceful Zebrafish
Fish Demands
Water Temperature 33 F to 80 F (1 C - 27 C)
Water Hardness 5 dGH to 12 dGH
pH Preferences pH 6.5 to pH 7.5
Minimum Tank Size 50 gallons
Feeding Comet goldfish feed on high-quality dried food, bloodworms, small insects, larvae, plant-based foods such as peas, lettuce and broccoli.

Estimated Price (RM): 4-5 per Comet Goldfish
