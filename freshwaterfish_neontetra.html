Neon Tetra

Basic Profile
Family Characidae
Origin Southeastern Colombia, Eastern Peru, Western Brazil
Physical Characteristics Neon Tetras have slender, torpedo-shaped bodies with a bright neon blue line running from their head to their adipose fin. They are also known for their vibrant, colorful appearance.
Full-grown Size 1.5 inches
Life Span 5 to 10 years
Diet Omnivore
Tank Level Middle dwellers
Compatibility
Aggressive level Tank Mates
Peaceful Zebrafish White Cloud Mountain Minnow Guppy Cherry Barb
Fish Demands
Water Temperature 68 F to 79 F (20 C to 26 C)
Water Hardness 5 dGH to 15 dGH
pH Preferences pH 4 to pH 7.5
Minimum Tank Size 10 gallons
Feeding Neon Tetras feed on small pellets, granules, fine flakes, as well as frozen brine shrimp, daphnia, and freeze-dried or frozen bloodworms.

Estimated Price (RM): 3-8 per Neon Tetra

Tip:

It's best to keep Neon Tetras in groups of at least six and to avoid larger Tetras, as they may see Neon Tetras as a meal at the first opportunity.
