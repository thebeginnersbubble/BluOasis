Team Introduction

Welcome to 🐟BluOasis🐠!

We are The Beginners Bubble, a team comprising Hui Yee Wong (Team Leader), Filicia Jing Xuan Wong, and Clara Ricky Chang. Our mission is to provide a straightforward platform for both beginners and experienced fishkeepers to access reliable information📖 on fish care, tank maintenance, and aquascaping🌱. BluOasis is designed to be a practical resource for creating and maintaining thriving aquariums.

We extend our gratitude💝 to our lecturer, Serena Sim, for her invaluable guidance throughout this project. Her expertise has played a key role🗝️ in shaping the development of this website.
