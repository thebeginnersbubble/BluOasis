Step-by-Step Tutorial

Below is a step-by-step guide to help you create your dream aquarium. Let's get started!

Step 1: Identify Your Budget & Lifestyle

You might be wondering about a few things, and that's totally okay! Feel free to ask yourself the following questions.

  1. How much are you comfortable spending on your aquarium setup?
  2. Are you hoping to have a fish tank that doubles as a beautiful decoration for your space?
  3. Do you have limited time for regular fish tank maintenance?

Step 2: Choose Between a Community Tank or a Species-Only Tank

If you’re going with a species-only tank, feel free to jump ahead to Step 3. But if you’re planning a community tank, it's important to think about how well your fish will get along. Make sure each species is a good match for the others, especially when it comes to the following factors.

  • Aggression level
  • Full-grown size
  • Type of freshwater fish
  • Water temperature
  • pH preferences

Step 3: Choosing the Right Fish for Your Tank Size

When choosing your fish, always keep in mind how big they’ll grow so you can make sure your tank gives them plenty of space to thrive.

Step 4: Choosing the Right Tank Size

Make sure your fish have plenty of space by using a Fish Tank Calculator to find the right tank size for them to thrive.

Step 5: Essential Care and Maintenance for Your Tank

Don’t forget to get ready for the essential care and maintenance of your tank! Here are some key things to consider:

  • Basic Equipment
  • Aquarium Additives
  • Water Parameters

Step 6: Understanding Your Fish’s Dietary Needs

Take some time to research the diet of the fish you’re thinking about, and make sure you can provide them with the right food they need.

Step 7: Unleash Your Creativity

This step is all about personal style and creativity. Feel free to enhance your aesthetic tank with water plants and DIY hideouts!
